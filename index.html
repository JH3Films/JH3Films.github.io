Services

From development to delivery, the making of every movie can be as dramatic as the film itself. We are here to make sure your hero's journey is a smooth yet valiant sail.

Development

Turn ideas into treatments and scripts, secure copyright protections, get projects funded by Taiwan's International Co-funding Program, and many more sources.

Production

Cast and crew onboarding, contracting and accounting, location scouting and securing, equipment and property rentals. All that is needed for a smooth shoot.

Post-Production

Editing, color grading, and sound design, we can deliver your visions in 4K HDR and DCP compliant with SMPTE standard. Promotional editing such as teasers and trailers is also available.

Distribution

Via various affiliated streamers, or your own OTT site dedicated to your needs, you are just one door knock away to showcase your works to millions worldwide.

Monetization

Recoup your hardwork via multiple models and sources such as TVOD, SVOD, and HVOD. Earn extra revenue streams with customizable paywall functions for your exclusive content.

Co-Production

Our roster of multilingual crew with credits such as Ang Li's Life of Pi (2012), Luc Besson's Lucy (2014), and Martin Scorsese's Silence (2016) can deliver the best version of your vision.
